Chris Farquer

I have lived all over the country and picked up a wide range of knowledge. I've learned how to weld, fabricate, work with circuits, graphic design, painting, drawing, model sculpturing, and mixed media in the arts. I have worked with several musicians doing production work and sound design. I am currently a student at UTSA where I began as a double major in Chemistry and Art, however I switched my major to Electrical Engineering when I built my first Theremin. I have always loved synthesizers and the nasty distorted and organic sounds that they can make. I plan to use my degree to design and build synths or to enter into the special effects industry doing animatronics and design.
