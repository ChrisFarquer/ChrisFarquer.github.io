CD Package Original Artwork

A Better Living Through Circuitry is experimental electronic musical duo, they incorporate elements of techno, classical, ambient, jazz, trip hop, electro, industrial, and dub into expansive, sample heavy tracks, which are often covered extensively with thick layers of sound taking on dark and luminous tones. New Kingdom is a thematic record about a post apocalyptical planet after war has killed off all life and is just left with barren landscapes.

The art work represents the theme of the record by showing the lifeless landscapes. The the back of the CD is a close up of the background of the front of the CD which ties them together. The "robot" is a representation and a play off of the group's name. The names of the songs are the stages of the planet's demise.

In the song, water can be heard crashing (which ties into the back picture) while the talking of killing another man and screams can be heard (which is a representation of the theme of the record).It is a remix of FSOL's Herd Killing I did using Live, Battery and tons of sampled material.
